Mission, Creed & Motto

Mission: To deliver safe, thrilling, and memorable rafting experiences.

Creed: “Gone Rafting” is our promise to embrace the river and respect the environment.

Motto: Your Enjoyment is Our Pleasure.

History

We started in 2005 as a small rafting company founded by Mr. Allan Ogwal.

Over the years, we grew into a trusted adventure provider.

Our growth has been fueled by passion for adventure and commitment to safety.

With experienced guides and a love for the river, we blend excitement with respect for nature.
